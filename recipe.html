Korean Spicy Ramyeon

Serves: 2 Prep time: 5 minutes Cook time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 packs instant ramyeon noodles (Shin Ramyeon recommended)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100 g sliced spam or pork belly
  • 2 stalks green onion, chopped
  • 1 cup kimchi, roughly chopped
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 4 cups water or chicken broth
  • Sesame seeds to garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring water or broth to a rolling boil in a medium pot. Add the ramyeon seasoning packets and gochujang and stir to combine.
  2. Add the spam or pork belly slices and kimchi. Simmer for 34 minutes until the meat is heated through.
  3. Add the noodles and cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent clumping.
  4. Crack the eggs directly into the pot. Cover and let cook for 12 minutes until the whites are set but yolks are still slightly runny.
  5. Top with cheese slices, green onion, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds. Serve immediately straight from the pot.

Jin's Notes

The cheese slice on top sounds unusual but it melts into the broth and adds a creamy richness that balances the spice perfectly. Don't skip it!

For an extra kick, add a spoonful of kimchi juice directly into the broth. If you're riding the next morning, maybe go easy on the heat!
